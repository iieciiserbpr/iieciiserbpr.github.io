What is Stream??

STREAM, hosted by the Publications and Outreach Council at IISER Berhampur, is a dynamic science outreach fest designed to ignite curiosity among local school students. Through hands-on activities, the event fosters a sense of wonder and exploration in various fields of science. By partnering with several academic clubs, each specializing in subjects like biology, physics, coding, and earth sciences, STREAM offers an immersive experience that makes learning science both enjoyable and practical.

This outreach initiative not only connects students to real-world applications of science but also emphasizes the importance of a scientific mindset in daily life. STREAM collaborates with local schools to extend its impact beyond the campus, encouraging students to explore science in a way that’s accessible and deeply engaging. With club-led activities, STREAM introduces students to topics they might not encounter in the standard curriculum, making science a hands-on adventure.



By cultivating an environment of collaborative learning, STREAM aims to inspire young minds to consider future careers in STEM fields. This experience strengthens the relationship between IISER and the community, creating a pathway for students to see science as a possible future profession. STREAM ultimately seeks to nurture a lifelong passion for discovery and innovation in young learners, setting the foundation for the next generation of scientific thinkers.
